Common Questions

  • Is my data private? Yes. Poker Dairy stores data locally on your device.
  • How do I export? Open Reports → tap the export icon → choose CSV.
  • How do I restore purchases? Open Settings → Restore Purchases.

Troubleshooting

  • If export fails, try again after reopening the app.
  • If a receipt scan doesn’t appear, confirm Photos/Camera permission in iOS Settings.
